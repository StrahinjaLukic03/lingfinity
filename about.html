About

Lingfinity is a free language learning platform developed by Amsakan Bavan and Strahinja Lukić with the aim of improving language learning and making it a more fun experience. Especially for less known languages such as Finnish & Serbian, because we thought there wasn’t enough optimal resources for people to be able to learn them. This project begun on 20th of July 2020 randomly out of nowhere in the middle of the day. The platform is mainly built on HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
